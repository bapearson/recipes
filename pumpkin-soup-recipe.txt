Pumpkin Soup

15-ounce can pumpkin
14-1/2 oz. can vegetable broth or chicken broth
1 cup fat free (or reduced fat?) milk
1 T. light brown sugar
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
½ tsp. ground ginger
¼ tsp. ground cloves
¼ tsp. salt
chives (optional)

Stir together pumpkin, broth and milk in a large saucepan.
Stir in brown sugar, cinnamon, ginger, cloves and salt; heat just to boiling.
Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, for 5 minutes.
To serve, pour into mugs; sprinkle with chives if desired.
